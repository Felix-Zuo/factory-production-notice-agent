# Code Of Conduct

This public demo is maintained as a professional portfolio project. Discussion
should stay technical, respectful, and focused on improving the repository.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear, specific issue and pull request descriptions.
- Keep examples synthetic or masked.
- Assume good intent, but correct inaccurate technical claims with evidence.
- Respect privacy boundaries around customer, supplier, site, and operational
  data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Posting private operational data, credentials, logs, or internal screenshots.
- Pressuring maintainers to expose private project context.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates these rules. Serious
or repeated violations may result in blocked participation in the repository.
